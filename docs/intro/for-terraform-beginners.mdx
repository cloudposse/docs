If you're a beginner with Terraform, you're in luck. As a blank slate, you won't have any bad habits to unlearn. Trust our experience—since 2015, we've been working almost exclusively with Terraform and have distilled our insights into this reference architecture.

Designed in partnership with numerous startups and enterprises, it’s flexible enough to meet unknown requirements. This Swiss Army Knife capability, paired with standardized conventions, best practices, comprehensive documentation, and strong community support, makes it incredibly powerful. We're so confident you'll love it that we offer free support to help you get started. Plus, we provide additional support options like twice-weekly customer workshops, weekly office hours, and custom professional services for advanced needs.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
- Start with our [Quick Start Guide](https://atmos.tools/quickstart) for Atmos, to learn the basics of the framework
- Learn how to [Build your foundation](/learn) (Organization, OUs Accounts, Network & VPCs, IAM & Single Sign-On)
- Set up your platform (Kubernetes, ECS, EKS, etc.)
- Deploy your apps (CI/CD, GitHub Actions, GitOps.)
- Monitor everything (Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana, etc. Also, monitoring for Security & Compliance)
- Upgrade & Maintain (Day-2 operations including upgrades, backups, disaster recovery, etc.)
</Steps>
