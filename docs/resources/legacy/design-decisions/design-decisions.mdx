<Intro>
  Design Decisions are architectural considerations for how to approach or
  implement some sort of functionality. The decision outcomes should be
  documented as Architectural Design Records (ADRs).
</Intro>

See [how to document a new design decision](/learn/maintenance/tutorials/how-to-document-a-new-design-decision) to this reference architecture as well as [How to write ADRs](/learn/maintenance/tutorials/how-to-write-adrs) .

:::note
This is our entire reference catalog of Design Decisions and not all may be relevant to the scope of a particular engagement.

:::
