<Intro>
  This documentation will guide you through the end-to-end configuration of our reference architecture for your AWS organization. You can customize everything to suit your needs and implement it at your own pace. Many have completed the process in under a week, and don't forget to join our weekly Customer Workshops for support if you get stuck.
</Intro>
